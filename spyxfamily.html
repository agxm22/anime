About the Anime

Spy × Family* follows the story of Loid Forger, an undercover spy tasked with an important mission that requires him to create a fake family. Unbeknownst to him, his "wife" Yor is an assassin, and his adopted daughter Anya has telepathic powers. Together, they try to live a "normal" family life while hiding their true identities.

Key Facts

  • Genre: Action, Comedy, Spy, Family
  • First aired: 2022
  • Based on the manga by Tatsuya Endo
  • Studio: Wit Studio & CloverWorks
